# Surveying AI Agents: Strengths and Limitations of LLMs and RAGs in Modern Applications

## 📌 Overview
This research paper aims to conduct a comprehensive evaluation of ChatGPT, Mistral, Grok, Llama, and DeepSeek, comparing their performance across key metrics and differences between their RAG systems. Additionally, we will examine how architectural advancements in newer iterations of these models contribute to their overall efficiency and reliability.
